## Design Notes

1. **Colors to consider:**
   - Primary: Trustworthy blue or green (money/growth)
   - Success: Green
   - Warning: Amber/Yellow
   - Alert: Red
   - Background: Light/White for clarity

2. **Typography:**
   - Headers: Bold, clear
   - Body: Easy to read
   - Numbers: Prominent, easy to scan

3. **Key UX principles:**
   - Minimal text, maximum clarity
   - One primary action per screen
   - Progress indicators everywhere
   - Celebratory moments for achievements

4. **Accessibility:**
   - Large touch targets
   - High contrast
   - Support for regional languages (Phase 2)
